Meet us

Meet our team of professionals

We believe in ongoing exploration and education. Our staff members are professionals with a wide-range of experiences and knowledge to find the right styles to suit you. Each member is given stupend funds to increase their experiences - because investing in our team is an investment for our guests.

Grace

Founder and Head Stylist

Grace lived in and trained in Hollywood for 7 years, working with celebrities before moving to South Hills to help co-found the Ayesha hair salon as the Head Stylist. Grace loves every bit of the home she's created.

Laura

Stylist & Colourist

Laura has lived in Vancouver her whole life and doesn't plan on moving any time soon. When not giving guests their best hair, Laura can be found hiking and climbing.

Ryan

Stylist

Having 3 sisters, Ryan has grown up knowing and understanding hair. Ryan has been a stylist servicing our guests for 3 years and absolutely loves it.

Amara

Stylist & Colourist

Born, raised and trained in Toronto, Amara has taken her talents here to explore the west coast. In her free-time, Amara loves to play her guitar and sing.

Meg

Stylist & Colourist

Meg loves to work with hair. Creating the perfect shade is Meg's specialty. From virgin hair to colour correction, she makes all heads of hair shine bright.
