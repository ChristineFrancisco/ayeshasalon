About us - South Hill's Premiere Hair Salon

It all started on March 1st 2008 when Grace decided that she wanted to go home after 7 years of servicing Hollywood. Her passion for hair began in 1998. She took in many young aspiring hair stylists to help grow and strengthen their careers in a family-style hair shop after becoming a mother. From then on, Ayesha Hair Salon has become a second home for barbers, sylists and clients. We care about everyone who walks through our doors

All of our stylists and colourists have their own unique style. The one thing everyone has in common is making sure all of our guests walk out with a boost of confidence! We are passionate about what we do and we thank everyone for comes to us to service them.

Love -Grace.
